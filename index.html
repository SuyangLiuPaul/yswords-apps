YsWords 雅伟之言

A bilingual Bible reader with AI-assisted word study, 13 translations, and a genuinely deep tool set for people who read the text closely.

Highlights

  • 13 translations — KJV, LEB, NASB, plus six 简体/繁體 Chinese versions including 和合本雅伟版
  • Split View — two independent reading panes, side by side or stacked, each its own version
  • AI word study — tap any verse for interlinear Strong's, then AI exegesis scoped from single-verse to whole-Bible
  • Bible Evidence — 225 archaeology / manuscript / history entries, cross-linked to scripture
  • Bible Trivia — 68 curated entries on acrostics, numeric patterns, and divine-name codes
  • Offline Pack — one-tap download of Bibles, sermons, tools, and maps for full offline reading
  • Cloud sync — optional Google sign-in syncs highlights, bookmarks, and notes across devices
  • Highlights, bookmarks & notes — 6 highlight colors, per-verse notes, persistent everywhere

News Insights 新闻洞见

World news, read alongside Scripture — every headline is automatically paired by AI with a Bible verse and a short reflection connecting the two.

Highlights

  • Bilingual throughout — every headline, summary, verse, and reflection in both English and 简体中文
  • World / China / Australia sections, filterable with one tap
  • Verse Lens card — the AI-picked verse, its text, a theme tag, and a reflection linking it to the story
  • Scroll into history — reach the bottom of today's edition and it keeps paging in previous days
  • Offline-first — the feed renders instantly from cache, then quietly refreshes in the background
  • Always links back — reads a short excerpt, then sends you to the original publisher for the full story

SeekSparks 寻光

A bilingual Bible exegesis tool for bigger screens — structured original-language search built for iPad web, Mac, and Windows.

Highlights

  • Structured Strong's search — combine original-language words with AND / OR / NOT / NEAR (word-proximity) and a prefix wildcard
  • Docked side panels — the word study opens as a persistent right-hand panel on desktop widths instead of covering the text
  • Wide-screen by default — Split View and the sidebar are already open at tablet width and up, no extra tap needed
  • Word-by-word study — Strong's number, transliteration, and gloss for every original-language word, Hebrew and Greek

About

YsWords, News Insights, and SeekSparks are built and maintained by one developer, Paul Liu, as free, ad-free tools for reading Scripture and staying informed.

Made by Paul Liu · Questions, feedback, or anything else: paul.sy.liu@gmail.com

News Insights reads from yswords-data, the same shared data pipeline built to serve YsWords — the AI verse-matching and translation engine behind both apps' Bible content. SeekSparks is a fork of YsWords, rebuilt for bigger screens with structured original-language search.

All three apps are free, open-source, and independently maintained. News content and images belong to their original publishers.
